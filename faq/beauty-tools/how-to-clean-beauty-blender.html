How do I clean a makeup sponge?

Short answerClean sponges after nearly every use. Wet the sponge, work in a solid or liquid cleanser, and gently squeeze under running water until it runs clear, then air-dry in an open spot. Damp sponges grow bacteria fast, so frequent cleaning and a fully dry storage spot are essential.

Because a sponge is damp and porous, it's the fastest of all your tools to harbour bacteria — so it needs the most frequent cleaning.

Quick method

  • Soak the sponge, then massage cleanser through it (solid brush/sponge bars work well).
  • Squeeze repeatedly under lukewarm water until no more product comes out and water runs clear.
  • Press out moisture in a clean towel and air-dry somewhere open — not sealed in a drawer or bag.

Reviewers single out solid cleansers as great for sponges specifically — "wonderful for both makeup brushes and sponges." Deep-clean weekly even if you rinse daily, and replace any sponge that won't come fully clean or starts to smell.
