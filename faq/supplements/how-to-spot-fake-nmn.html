How can I avoid fake or low-quality NMN?

Short answerBuy from brands that publish third-party purity tests (look for ~98–99%+ purity), avoid prices that seem too good to be true, and be wary of unknown marketplace sellers. NMN is also heat-sensitive, so storage and shipping matter. Community members repeatedly warn that fake NMN is common online.

NMN's popularity has attracted counterfeits and underdosed products, a recurring warning in the r/NMN community.

How to protect yourself

  • Demand testing: reputable brands publish third-party Certificates of Analysis; look for stated purity around 98–99%+.
  • Price sanity: suspiciously cheap bulk NMN is a red flag — "too good to be true" usually is.
  • Seller trust: buy from the brand or an authorised seller, not random marketplace listings.
  • Handling: NMN can degrade with heat — one reviewer flagged a bottle that "arrived very hot," unsure if it was still good.

As one redditor put it while eyeing a cut-price brand, "$140 for 100 grams seems too good to be true — and you know what they say about that feeling."
