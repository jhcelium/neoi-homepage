Our Story

Quiet luxury, made in Japan

Neoi began with a simple belief: that the things we use every day — what we breathe, what we put on our skin, what we take for our health — deserve the same patience and precision Japan brings to its craft.

Made with intention

From organic essential oils and reed diffusers to hand-finished makeup brushes and meticulously formulated supplements, every Neoi product is sourced and made with care. We work with domestic Japanese manufacturers and certified facilities so that quality is never left to chance.

We don't chase trends. We make calm, considered products designed to last — and to fit quietly into the rhythm of a good life.

What we stand for

Meticulous sourcing

Premium ingredients selected for purity and provenance — organic-certified oils, domestically sourced actives, nothing superfluous.

Certified quality

Manufactured in GMP-certified, ISO 9001 facilities in Japan, with rigorous quality systems behind every batch.

Craft & calm

Artisan beauty tools and considered design — the unhurried, precise making that Japan is known for.
