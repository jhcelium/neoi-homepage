Frequently asked questions

Where are Neoi products made?
Every Neoi product is domestically manufactured in Japan. Our essential oils, beauty tools and supplements are produced in GMP-certified, ISO 9001-managed facilities with meticulously sourced ingredients.

Are your supplements certified?
Yes. Our supplements are produced in facilities certified to GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) and ISO 9001 quality-management standards, ensuring consistent quality and safety in every batch.

Are your essential oils pure and natural?
Our aromatherapy range is crafted from carefully sourced botanical ingredients and blended with the calm and precision of Japanese making. Explore the full collection on our beauty store.

Do you ship internationally?
Shipping options vary by collection and destination. Please visit the relevant beauty or supplement store, or contact us, for the most up-to-date shipping and availability details for your region.

How do I care for my Neoi makeup brushes?
Our artisan brushes are made with natural bristles and reward gentle care. See our guide on caring for makeup brushes for step-by-step cleaning and drying tips that preserve their shape and longevity.

How can I get in touch with Neoi?
You can reach us by email at neoi.jp.mail@gmail.com or through our contact page. We are based in Osaka, Japan.
